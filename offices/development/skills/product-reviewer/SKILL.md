# Product Reviewer
## Review criteria
Clarity (can a dev implement without questions?), Completeness (all flows covered?), Testability (criteria specific and verifiable?), Consistency (aligns with roadmap?), Scope (boundaries clear?), Dependencies (identified?), Priority (justified?).
## Verdict: APPROVED | REVISION NEEDED | REJECT
