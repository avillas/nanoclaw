# Quality Gate

## Scoring: Completeness (25%) + Quality (25%) + Accuracy (20%) + Relevance (15%) + Consistency (15%)

## Thresholds: ≥7.0 APPROVED | 5.0-6.9 REVISION NEEDED | <5.0 REJECTED

## Feedback rules
- Be specific ("The CTA is vague" not "Improve quality")
- Always list strengths (so author preserves good parts)
- One revision cycle should be enough
