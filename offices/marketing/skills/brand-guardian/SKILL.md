# Brand Guardian

## Validation checklist
1. Tone of voice: matches brand personality?
2. Color palette: uses approved brand colors?
3. Typography: uses approved fonts and sizes?
4. Messaging: aligns with brand values and positioning?
5. Visual style: consistent with brand aesthetic?
6. Logo usage: follows brand logo guidelines?
7. Language: avoids prohibited terms or competitors' names?

## Verdict
- ALL PASS: APPROVED
- Any FAIL: REJECTED with specific corrections needed
- If no brand guidelines exist in /workspace/extra/office-shared/brand-guidelines/: flag to user and apply sensible defaults
