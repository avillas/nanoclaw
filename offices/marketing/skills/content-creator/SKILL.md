# Content Creator

Skill for creating and reviewing digital marketing campaign content.

## When to use

- Writing a new campaign script from a theme or brief
- Reviewing an existing campaign against quality criteria
- Adapting content for different platforms or audiences

## Campaign creation process

### Step 1 — Research (mandatory)

Search for 3-5 sources on the campaign theme. Focus on recent trends, audience sentiment, and competitor approaches.

### Step 2 — Structure

Every campaign script follows: Hook (first 3 seconds) → Body (3-5 key points) → CTA (one clear action).

### Step 3 — Platform adaptation

| Format | Guidelines |
|--------|-----------|
| Feed post | Max 2,200 chars. Visual-first. Strong first line. |
| Stories | 15s per slide. Tap-through narrative. Max 5-7 slides. |
| Reels | 15-60s. Hook in first 1.5s. Vertical 9:16. |
| Carousel | 5-10 slides. Title slide must hook. Last slide = CTA. |

### Step 4 — Hashtag strategy

Select 10-15 hashtags in three tiers:
- High volume (3-5): 100k+ posts
- Mid volume (4-5): 10k-100k posts
- Niche (3-5): Under 10k posts

## Review criteria

Score each dimension 1-10: Hook strength (20%), Body value (25%), CTA clarity (15%), Audience fit (15%), Platform fit (10%), Brand alignment (10%), Research depth (5%).

Thresholds: ≥7.0 APPROVED | 5.0-6.9 REVISION NEEDED | <5.0 REJECT

## Guidelines

- Research is non-negotiable
- Specificity beats generality
- One CTA per campaign
- Hook is the highest-leverage element
