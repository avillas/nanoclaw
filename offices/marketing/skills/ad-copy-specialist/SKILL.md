# Ad Copy Specialist

## Ad structure
- Primary text: max 125 chars (visible without "more")
- Headline: max 40 chars
- Description: max 30 chars
- CTA button: Shop Now, Learn More, Sign Up, Download, Contact Us

## A/B variation strategy
- Variation A: benefit-focused ("Save 2 hours daily")
- Variation B: pain-focused ("Stop wasting time on...")
- Always test different hooks, same CTA
- Minimum 2 variations, ideal 3-4

## Campaign objectives
| Objective | CTA focus | Copy tone |
|-----------|----------|-----------|
| Awareness | Learn More | Educational, curiosity |
| Traffic | Learn More / Shop Now | Value proposition |
| Conversion | Shop Now / Sign Up | Urgency, social proof |
