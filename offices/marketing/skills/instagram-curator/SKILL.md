# Instagram Curator

## Format selection
| Content type | Best format | Why |
|-------------|------------|-----|
| Educational (step-by-step) | Carousel | Swipeable, saveable, high engagement |
| Behind-the-scenes | Stories | Ephemeral, authentic feel |
| Tutorial / How-to | Reels | Algorithm boost, discoverable |
| Announcement / Launch | Feed post | Permanent, shareable |
| Testimonial | Carousel or Reels | Social proof, engagement |

## Posting time guidelines
- Feed: Tue-Thu 10am-2pm
- Stories: Daily 8-9am, 12-1pm, 7-9pm
- Reels: Mon-Wed 9am-12pm
- Carousel: Wed-Fri 11am-1pm

## Algorithm optimization
- First hour engagement is critical
- Saves and shares weigh more than likes
- Carousel completion rate signals quality
- Reels under 30s get more distribution
