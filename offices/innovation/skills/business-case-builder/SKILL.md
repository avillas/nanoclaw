# Business Case Builder
## Market sizing
- TAM: total addressable market (everyone who could use this)
- SAM: serviceable addressable market (within our reach)
- SOM: serviceable obtainable market (realistic year 1-2 capture)
- Methodology: top-down (industry reports) or bottom-up (unit economics)
## Revenue model options
Subscription, transaction fee, freemium, marketplace commission, licensing, advertising
## MVP scope
- Must have (launch blockers)
- Should have (week 2-4 additions)
- Nice to have (backlog)
